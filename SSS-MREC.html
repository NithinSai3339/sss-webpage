Vaccination Drive


We have decided to plan a vaccination camp in MREC and executed successfully on December 7th and the camp lasted up to 9th of December. It is one of the biggest EVENTS as well as one of the biggest achievements of SSS. We have vaccinated more than 900 people. The event was held in Malla Reddy Engineering College (MREC), Hyderabad. Our special Thanks to this event were Uday Bhaskar sir and Chitti Babu sir and the entire College management for the permission to conduct such a big camp. The initiate of vaccination camp took by Ajay Kumar (Executive member of SSS).
